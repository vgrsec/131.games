Administratum Administration

This section is for Administratum Administrators to know what activities are needed to keep the crusade running

Planetary Control Points

Each victor must record their game in administratum in order for their faction to record the victory.

As admin, periodically (once a week) scroll through the games recorded. If a game recorded has a defeated but not a victory, ping the victor to record their game in administratum. Feel free to provide this link To record a game in administratum
