About

This crusade is open to all Warhammer 40k players. Players will be able to join throughout the crusade and will be assigned teams. Off the Wall discord will be the primary place to communicate crusade news. The crusade will run from September 2022 to March 2023.

Overview

Echoes from the Void is a Warhammer 40k Crusade League campaign by Bolters at Dawn used with permission. The crusade takes place over five chapters of faction vs. faction warfare. The forces of the Imperium clash with the traitors of Chaos along side the cunning Xenos factions as they battle for faction-wide bonuses and accomplishing chapter-specific objectives. The story takes place in the system of Huthar; a derelict, long-lost system near Vordrast in the Segmentum Solar sector.

Factions at War

The armies of Imperium, Chaos, and Xenos will clash and battle eachother to accomplish victory in Huthar. This is done by winning Planetary Control within each Chapter. Players will be split in to 3 player teams largely based on faction. Mixed faction teams will be allowed if supported by narrative.

Code of Conduct

Treat people with respect. Exhibit sportsmanship. Respect the game. Honesty at all times. Lead by example.

Violation of this code of conduct can result in expulsion from the crusade. Please do not behave in such a way where we need to make that decision

Treat people with respect

Participants in the crusade are expected to treat all people around them with respect at all times. This includes but is not limited to fellow crusade players, other people in the store, and store staff. Individuals may want to be treated a little different than you want to be treated, this is ok, respect their wishes.

Respecting people also means following the rules of the places you play. By following the rules of the places we play, we demonstrate respect to the staff who provide the space.

Exhibit sportsmanship

Games can become emotional events for everyone, we want everyone to have a positive time at our games. Being a sore winner or loser is not acceptable.

Respect the game

Playing games involves trust. We trust that everyone respects the game and follows the rules to the best of their ability. Cheating will not be tollerated. This includes using loaded dice, lying about rules that affect your army, or any number of dishonest behavior.

Honesty at all times

Mistakes happen, rules change and we miss it, dice fall off the table. If you make an honest mistake make attempts to correct for it as quickly as possible.

Lead by example.

Bad behaivor by others doesn't excuse your own bad behavior. If someone is behaving badly around you, use your judgement on how to proceed, but retaliation and bad behavior is never acceptable.
